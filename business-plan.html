What We Do

We are an F&B consulting practice built specifically for senior living operators. Dining is the largest controllable cost in most senior living buildings — and the most neglected. We come in, identify the inefficiencies, and fix them.

Our target is an 8–12% improvement in overall dining operations for every client we take on — measured through food cost reduction, labor efficiency, resident satisfaction, and vendor savings.

The Core Pitch We pay for ourselves. The savings and improvements we identify in the first 90 days consistently exceed the cost of the engagement.

The Opportunity

Demand for senior housing is outpacing supply. Acquisition activity is rising as investor groups buy distressed assets and need immediate operational support. Most operators have no dedicated F&B expertise — dining is the problem they don't know how to solve.

We are launching in Birmingham, AL and the surrounding metro — a concentrated market of assisted living, memory care, and skilled nursing facilities within 30 minutes of each other. No dominant local F&B consulting presence exists.

Primary Market — AL / MC / IL

  • Keep dining in-house, use consultants
  • Need cost control, menu quality, weekly reporting
  • Monthly retainer model

High-Value Target — SNF Portfolios

  • Jewish Orthodox-owned groups contract out 100% of dining
  • Full management fee — staffing, food, menus, POS
  • Primarily NY and CA-based, active in Southeast

  • Referral partners — operators, PE acquirers, and regional VPs already in the ecosystem. One warm intro is worth 50 cold emails.
  • Direct outreach — targeted to Executive Directors and Regional Directors of Ops within 150-mile radius
  • NIC Fall Conference, Chicago (October 2026) — the premier senior housing event. We have an active referral partner (Umbredo Living, Birmingham) attending and carrying our capabilities deck
